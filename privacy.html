Privacy Policy

We are committed to privacy issues, and this privacy policy details our approach. By using this site and submitting any personal data, you agree to the use by Hex Creative of such data in accordance with this privacy statement.

Data Collection

The collection of identifiable personal data or information that is specifically and voluntarily provided by a visitor to our site may include name and contact information provided in order to:

  • Order a site development package
  • Contact us for further information

We also use personal data from relevant trade directories and from individuals who we have met.

We do not hold any of the special categories of sensitive data.

Data Controller

The data controller for the purposes of data protection legislation is Hex Creative.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a database which can only be accessed by our marketing function and IT team (to maintain the database and to monitor and update security measures). We take every appropriate measure to ensure that your data is safely and securely stored. We will keep your data on file for as long as we continue to send you information on products or services that may be relevant to you. If you request that we stop sending you such communications, we will hold your data on a “do not send” list to give effect to your wishes.

Data Sharing

We do not share data with any companies or individuals

Data Use

We use the data collected to keep in touch with clients, potential clients and other interested parties about current products and services. Under the grounds of it being in the legitimate interests of Hex Creative to do so. However, we always balance need for communication and marketing with your need for privacy and control of your data. we will not send excessively frequent communications, and you always have the right to ask us to stop sending you marketing communications.
